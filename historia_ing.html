The ASIFIM Method

The ASIFIM Method combines physical, mental, emotional, energetic, and spiritual self-exploration, using the disciplines we teach as the main tool. This approach allows practitioners to:

  • Adapt and personalize the disciplines according to their needs and personal goals.
  • Develop a "personal discipline" based on innovation and adaptation.

Inspired by Eastern philosophies and modern techniques, the ASIFIM Method promotes self-discovery and holistic balance, helping participants unlock their full potential.
